Heart Health

80% of cardiovascular diseases are preventable. 1 in 4 deaths in the US is due to heart disease. 1 in 3 adults deals with hypertension.

Risk Factors for heart

  • Smoking
  • Physical inactivity
  • Unhealthy Diet
  • Excessive Alcohol Consumption
  • Stress
  • Family History
  • Age(65+)
  • Ethnicity(African Americans,Hispanis)
  • Socioeconomic Status
