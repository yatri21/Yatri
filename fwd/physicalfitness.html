Benefits

Weight Management
Improved Mental Health
Reduced Chronic Disease Risk (heart disease, diabetes)
Enhanced Athletic Performance
Better Sleep Quality

Exercise Types

  • Aerobic: jogging, cycling, dancing
  • Resistance Training: weightlifting, bodyweight exercises
  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
  • Yoga and Pilates for flexibility and core strength
  • Group Fitness Classes (e.g., Zumba, spinning)

Common Fitness Mistakes

  1. Inadequate warm-up/cool-down
  2. Insufficient rest and recovery
  3. Poor form and technique
  4. Overtraining
  5. Lack of variety in workouts
