Key Components of a Healthcare System:


1. Healthcare Providers: Doctors, nurses, and allied health professionals.
2. Healthcare Facilities: Hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, and community health centers.
3. Health Insurance: Private, public, or mixed models to finance healthcare.
4. Healthcare Administration: Government agencies, regulatory bodies, and health information systems.
5. Patients and Communities: Recipients of healthcare services.
    Functions of a Healthcare System:
  • Health Promotion: Education, prevention, and early intervention.
  • Disease Prevention: Vaccinations, screenings, and health checks. Diagnosis and Treatment: Medical care for acute and chronic conditions.
  • Rehabilitation: Restoring health and function after illness or injury.
  • Palliative Care: Support for patients with life-limiting conditions.
