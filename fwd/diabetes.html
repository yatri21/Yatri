Diabetes Management

463 million people live with diabetes worldwide. 1 in 11 adults have diabetes. Diabetes is the 9th leading cause of death globally. 75% of people with diabetes live in low-middle-income countries.

Strategies for Diabetes Management

Medication adherence Taking prescribed medications as directed
Blood glucose monitoring Regularly tracking blood sugar levels
Healthy Eating Balanced Diet, Carbohydrate counting and meal planning
Physical Activity Regular Exercise(Walking,aerobic)
Weight Management Maintaining Healthy weight
Stress management Yoga, meditation or cognitive-behaviour therapy
Sleep Management 7-8 hours of sleep per night
Tobacco cessation quitting smoking
Regular Health check-ups Monitorig Kidney function Blood pressure

Complication prevention is also necessary:

  1. Nephropathy(kidney disease) Prevention
  2. Retinopathy(eye damage) Prevention
  3. Neuropathy(Nerve Damage) Prevention
  4. Foot Care
  5. Cardiovascular disease prevention

Monitoring and Testing

  1. Fasting Plasma Glucose(FPG) test
  2. Oral glucose tolerance test(OGTT)
  3. Hemoglobin A1c(HbA1c) test
  4. Urine tests(ketones,protien)
  5. Blood pressure monitoring
