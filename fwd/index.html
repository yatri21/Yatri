Health Care


Health care is the improvement of health, diagnosis, treatment, amelioration or cure of disease, illness, injury, and other physical and mental impairments in people.A good health is a state of complete physical mental and social well-being.
It plays a key role in a person's performance.A healthcare system is an organization of people, institutions, and resources that deliver healthcare services to meet the health needs of a population.

Overview to health care system


A healthcare system is an organized network of healthcare providers, insurance companies, hospitals, clinics, and government agencies that work together to deliver essential healthcare services to individuals and communities.
    Types of Healthcare Systems:
  1. Single-Payer System (e.g., UK, Canada
  2. Multi-Payer System (e.g., US, Germany)
  3. Mixed System (e.g., France, Australia)
  4. National Health Insurance (NHI) System (e.g., Taiwan, South Korea)

Public health focuses on the health of populations rather than individuals. It involves efforts to prevent disease, promote health, and prolong life through organized community efforts.
