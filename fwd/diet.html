Types of Diets:
  1. Mediterranean Diet (emphasizes whole grains, fruits, vegetables, lean protein)
  2. Keto Diet (high-fat, low-carbohydrate)
  3. Vegan Diet (plant-based, no animal products)
  4. Gluten-Free Diet (for gluten intolerance or sensitivity
  5. Low-Carb Diet (reduces carbohydrate intake)
  6. Intermittent Fasting (alternating periods of eating and fasting)
  7. Paleo Diet (focuses on whole, unprocessed foods)
  8. Whole30 Diet (eliminates grains, dairy, processed foods)
  9. Atkins Diet (low-carbohydrate, high-protein)
  10. DASH Diet (emphasizes whole grains, fruits, vegetables, lean protein)

Nutrition

  1. Incorporate omega-3 rich foods (salmon, walnuts)
  2. Eat antioxidant-rich foods (berries, leafy greens)
  3. Include probiotics (yogurt, kimchi)
  4. Choose whole grains over refined carbs
  5. Limit processed and sugary foods
  6. Drink green tea or herbal tea
  7. Cook with healthy oils (olive, avocado)
